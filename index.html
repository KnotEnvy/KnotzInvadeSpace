Knotz: Invade Space

A free story-driven arcade space shooter roguelite you can play in your browser. Steal an advanced alien ship, fight home through a five-sector campaign to break the siege of Earth, dock with the carrier UES Orion to spend banked credits on permanent upgrades, and test yourself in Endless mode and seeded Daily Challenges. Keyboard, mouse and touch supported. No install, no account — just play.
